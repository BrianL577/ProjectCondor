How the index is calculated

Each weekly report is scored 0–100 from five variables already tracked per location: competitive position (Signature Position — dominant/leading/competitive/marginal, 40%), positional ranking (#1 of N operators, 15%), market share (estimate or qualitative tier, 20%), market-signal momentum (net positive vs negative signals that week, 15%), and data confidence (share of KPIs with verified values, 10%). The portfolio index is the average across all in-scope locations for that week, rebased to 100 at the first tracked week — so it reads like a stock chart: above 100 means the portfolio is stronger than when tracking began.

Validation status

The links above are the authoritative sources used to generate and verify each report. KPI rows the generator cannot confirm against a named source are now omitted from report views rather than shown as "Not available" — anything unverifiable is listed under "Requires manual verification" inside the report instead. Flagship facts for VNY and TEB (operations counts, terminal counts, competitor lists) were confirmed directly against LAWA, the Port Authority of NY&NJ, Signature's press room, and AIN. Remaining per-location baseline estimates in scripts/generate.js (market share, EBITDA context) are clearly labeled as estimates in each report — spot-check against the links above before relying on one for a decision.
